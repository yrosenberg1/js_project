    About

    As a baseball fan, I thought it would be cool to be able to see how your favorite player's baseball statistics compares with the league overall. MLBStats allows you to plot a chart and see how your favorite players stacks up overall by either percentile or ranking overall.


    You can either enter a player's name in the search bar to see how he performed, and you can select a season between 2016 and 2020 to see how he did in a certain season, or if you're feeling lucky you can select the randomizer button to see any player, or finally, you can choose a team from the dropdown and choose a player by team.


    You can also see this information again by clicking the 'About' Section.


    Enjoy!


    All the stats and data are courtesy of Baseball Reference.
